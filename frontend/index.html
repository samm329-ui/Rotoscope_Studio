Welcome to Rotoscope Studio

Rotoscope Studio is a standalone tool that helps you upload a video, separate the subject from the background, and export the result.

Choose a fast matte result for quick previews, or a precise rotoscope result for cleaner separation. You can compare both before exporting.

Upload a video

Drag and drop a video file, or use the browse button. Supported formats: MP4, MOV, AVI, WebM, MKV.
